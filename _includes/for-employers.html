Blue365 For Employers

Encourage your Employees to join Blue365 to build a culture of health and wellness at your workplace. Gain access to bulk discounts on wearable devices and more.

Why Blue365?

Reduce Health Care Costs

Investment in employees social, mental, and physical health pays off, with an estimated return of $2.71 for every dollar spent on wellness programs.
– Harvard Business Review, 2010

Increase Employee Productivity

Promoting wearables in the workplace increases productivity by as much as 8.5% and increases employee satisfaction by 3.5%.
– Cognizant, 2016

Great Products, Great Deals

The collective buying power of BCBS brings you better discounts than other health savings programs from premium brands that you and your employees recognize.

And it‘s all Free to BCBS Members

Blue365 is a turnkey benefit for existing members of a BCBS plan. It is easy to administer, there are no savings limits, no setup fees, and a no commitment registration process for employees.

Motivate, Incentivize and Empower with Blue365

Your employees can again access to exclusive health and wellness deals by registering with Blue365. It’s easy to join — in minutes, new members can start browsing deals that encourage a healthy lifestyle.

Take Advantage of Bulk Discounts

Large and small companies have access to exclusive bulk order discounts on wearable devices through Blue365 and Wellness Codes. Additionally, employers can take advantage of bulk order discounts on health monitors, healthy eating, and office health products.

Incentivize with Wellness Vouchers

Interested in subsidizing the cost of wellness products? Wellness vouchers are a great solution for any type of rewards or incentive program aimed at promoting healthy lifestyle changes.
